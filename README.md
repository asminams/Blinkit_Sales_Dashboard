# Blinkit_Sales_Dashboard
**Project Overview**

_This project analyzes the sales performance of Blinkit, India's prominent quick-commerce grocery platform.The data set includes details on associated outlets, item characteristics and customer ratings._ 

_The objective is to understand the trend in sales pattern and key metrics in relation to outlet type, size and location thus enabling informed business recommendations._


**KPI Definitions**

_The dataset focuses on following attributes:_

_Total Sales: Total revenue generated from all items sold_ 

_Average Sales: Average revenue per sale_ 

_No. of Items: Total count of items sold_ 

_Average rating: Average customer rating_ 


**Steps Performed**

_Handled null values in Item weight by fill option in transform header._

_Standardized Fat content options using replace function._

_Screened for formatting and value errors such as ensuring establishment year data type in whole number._

_Created a new column with ADD column function and inputting required formula_


**Tools Used**

_Excel: Initial Exploration & Power Query mode_

_Power BI: Dashboard creation & Visualization_

**Key Insights**

_1. Total Sales for the period 2011-2022 amounts to Rs 1.20 Mio._

_2. Average revenue per sales is Rs 141 approx._

_3. No. of Items sold is 8523._

_4. Average rating is 3.92_

_5. Medium sized outlets accounts for highest sales constituting 42.27% of sales._

_6. Tier 3 location tops the performance among Outlet location type with Rs 472.13K sales._

_7. Low Fat Items are sold in higher (approx. 65% of sales) as compared to Regular Fat content._

_8. Fruits and Vegetables along with Snack foods are the Item type mostly sold._

_9. Outlet Age seems to have low relation to sales trend. Sales chart in relation to establishment year is more or less stable._

_10. Supermarket Type 1 across outlet location accounts for major sales._

_11. Customer rating remained stable across metrics_

**Business Recommendations**

_* Inventory for low fat items to be boosted in comparison to regular fat content across outlet location._

_* Inventory for Fruits and Vegetables along with Snack foods to be maintained in higher amounts across locations._

_* Small outlet size in Tier 1 and Tier 2 locations contribute better where as medium sized outlets in Tier 3 impacts highly on total sales._

_* Marketing strategies in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities to be enhanced._

_* Association with all outlet types in Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities have to be increased._

_* Customer satisfaction measures such as fast & reliable service, quality, customer support etc. to continue with feedback guidelines._
